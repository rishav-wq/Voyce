FAQ

Questions? Answered.

Will the posts actually sound like me?

Yes — that's the core of it. Upload your past LinkedIn posts and Voyce learns your rhythm, vocabulary, and tone, so what it writes reads like you wrote it, not generic AI.

Does it post automatically, or do I approve first?

Both. On free you generate and post manually. On Pro, Voyce publishes on your daily schedule automatically — you can pause or edit any profile anytime.

What do I get for free?

5 generations to try — text posts, carousels, and image posts — plus manual posting to LinkedIn. No credit card required.

Is my content private?

Yes. Your profile data and posts are yours. Voyce only uses them to generate your content.

Can I cancel anytime?

There's nothing to cancel — Pro is a 30-day pass with no auto-charge. You renew only when you want to.

Does it work for a company page too?

Yes. Create separate company and personal profiles, each with its own style, schedule, and topics.
