# Voyce — 3-Week Demand Validation Sprint

**Goal:** 5 paying consultants + their verbatim answer to "would you let it post unattended?" — within 3 weeks.
**Why:** this is the only thing that moves the panel past 8/12 and tells you whether autopilot is the right hero. No code, no ads, no funnel. Concierge + your own LinkedIn.

**The product already supports this** — preview/dry-run mode means you can show a week of posts without publishing anything. Nothing to build.

---

## Week 1 — Prove it on yourself + build the list

### Step 1: You are user #1
- Arm Voyce on Rishav's own LinkedIn, autopilot on, consultant-positioned.
- Post daily. This is simultaneously your proof asset, your demo, and your warmest lead source (people asking "how are you posting daily?" are pre-qualified).

### Step 2: Build a list of 40 target consultants
Screen for all three: (a) gets or wants clients from LinkedIn, (b) posts *inconsistently* (active enough to care, erratic enough to need it), (c) solo — no marketing team.

| Source | How to find them | Target |
|---|---|---|
| Your 1st/2nd-degree network | LinkedIn search your connections for title: "fractional", "consultant", "advisor", "coach". Prioritize ones who already post occasionally. | 15 |
| LinkedIn cold (2nd/3rd degree) | Search "fractional CMO/CFO/COO", "independent consultant", "solo consultant". Filter to people who post ~1–4×/month (the inconsistency is the buying signal). | 15 |
| Communities | Fractionals United, r/consulting, r/Entrepreneur, solopreneur Slacks/Discords, On Deck alumni networks. Look for people who *complain* about visibility or "I should post more." | 10 |

Log each into the tracker (bottom of this doc) before contacting.

## Week 2 — Concierge offer to 20–30 of them

Send the message below. The offer *is* the trust mitigation: you do the setup, they see a week of posts, nothing goes live without their OK.

**Goal: 8–10 activated** (Voyce generating in their voice, them seeing real output).

### LinkedIn DM (warm / 1st-degree)
> Hi {First} — I built a tool for a problem I think you'll recognize: you get inbound from LinkedIn, but between client work, posting consistently is the first thing that slips.
>
> Voyce learns your writing voice and posts for you daily — pulling real news in your space so it's never generic filler. I'm hand-onboarding a few founding users.
>
> Can I set it up in your voice and show you a week of posts it *would* publish? Free, and nothing goes live without your OK. If you like it, it's $29/mo locked as a founding member. No pitch — I just want you to see the output.

### Email (cold / 2nd-degree)
> **Subject: keeping your LinkedIn from going quiet (without writing it)**
>
> Hi {First},
>
> Quick one — you clearly get value from LinkedIn, but if you're like most consultants I talk to, posting consistently is what slips when client work picks up.
>
> I built Voyce for exactly that: it learns your voice from your past posts, finds today's relevant news in your niche, and posts in your voice every day, on autopilot. The "never go quiet again" button.
>
> I'm hand-onboarding a handful of founding users. I'll set it up in your voice and show you a full week of posts it *would* publish — free, nothing goes live unless you say so. If it's good, $29/mo as a founding member, that price locked for you.
>
> Worth a 15-min setup? I do the work; you just tell me if the posts sound like you.
>
> — Rishav
> {link}

### Follow-up (day 3–4, if no reply)
> Hi {First} — floating this back up. Happy to just send you one sample post in your voice first, so you can judge the quality before spending any time. Want me to?

## Week 3 — Convert to paid + harvest objections

Move activated users to paying at $29 (founding-member framing). For **every** person, ask these two questions and record the **verbatim** answer — the wording is the data:

1. "Would you pay $29/mo for this?"
2. "Would you let it post *without* you approving each one first?"

**Goal: 5 paying consultants.**

---

## The decision gate (what the outcomes mean)

| Outcome | Reading | Next move |
|---|---|---|
| ≥5 paid + most OK with unattended | Demand + trust validated. Customer score 🟡→🟢, panel ~10/12 | Build scale channels: `/money-seo` + `/money-content` |
| They pay but won't allow unattended | Autopilot is the wrong hero | Approval queue becomes priority #1; reposition to "one-tap approve" |
| They won't pay $29 | It's trust or ICP, not price | Read the objections; may need `/money-review-customer` or a different ICP |
