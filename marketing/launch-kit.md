## 1. Standard form answers (paste these everywhere)

**Product name:** Voyce

**Website:** https://voyce.co.in

**Tagline (short, <60 chars):**
> LinkedIn posts that sound like you, not AI slop.

**Alt taglines (if the first is taken/too long):**
> Your voice, your stories, posted daily on LinkedIn.
> AI LinkedIn content in your own voice, on autopilot.

**Short description (~160 chars):**
> Voyce learns your voice from your past posts and turns rough thoughts into LinkedIn posts,
> branded carousels, and image posts that actually sound like you.

**Long description (~450 chars):**
> Every founder knows they should post on LinkedIn. Almost none do, and the AI tools that
> "help" make everyone sound identical. Voyce learns your voice from screenshots or text of
> your past posts, then turns rough ideas into LinkedIn posts, branded PDF carousels, and
> on-brand image posts that sound like you wrote them. Built on research into what actually
> earns reach: strong hooks, human rhythm, no engagement bait, none of the usual AI tells.
> Free to try; Pro adds unlimited generations and daily auto-posting.

**Feature bullets:**
- Learns your writing voice from screenshots or pasted text of 2-3 past posts
- Text posts, branded PDF carousels, and AI image posts from one rough idea
- Prompt rules built from research into what gets reach on LinkedIn (and what gets demoted)
- Kills the AI tells: no em-dashes, no "it's not X, it's Y", no engagement bait
- Daily auto-posting with a rotating post-style calendar (Pro)
- Brand-matched carousel themes (or auto-matched from your industry/website)

**Categories/tags:** AI writing assistant · Social media · LinkedIn · Content generation ·
Marketing · Personal branding · Copywriting

**Pricing:** Freemium — Free: 5 generations. Pro: ₹1,599/month (~$19): unlimited generations,
daily auto-posting, automated carousels.

**Founder:** Rishav Raj — https://www.linkedin.com/in/rishav-raj-177610262
**Contact email:** rishav@knowella.com   (or the founder email you prefer public)

**Maker story (for Peerlist / Product Hunt / Indie Hackers):**
> I'm an AI/ML engineer who could ship a 95%-retrieval RAG pipeline but froze every time I
> had to write a LinkedIn post. Two years of "I should post more" and not doing it. So I
> built Voyce: it learns your voice from your past posts and does the writing part, so
> showing up daily stops being the hard part. I'm building it solo and in public — the tool
> writes my build-in-public posts about itself.
